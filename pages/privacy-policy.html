Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. Learn how we collect, use, and protect your data.

Last updated: August 19, 2025

Information We Collect

At RUNSTR, we believe in minimal data collection and local-first storage. We only collect information that is essential for providing our fitness platform services:

Account Information

  • Username and display name
  • Email address for account verification
  • Profile picture (optional)
  • Nostr public key (for decentralized identity)

Fitness Data

  • Running distance, time, and pace
  • Challenge participation and results
  • Team memberships and created events
  • Achievement and streak data

Technical Information

  • Device type and operating system (for app compatibility)
  • App usage statistics (anonymous)
  • Bitcoin Lightning wallet connection status

Local-First Approach

Most of your data is stored locally on your device. We only sync essential information needed for competitions and team features to our servers.

How We Use Information

We use your information solely to provide and improve our fitness platform services:

  • Platform Operation: Enable core features like run tracking, teams, and competitions
  • Competition Management: Verify results and distribute prizes fairly
  • Community Features: Connect you with teams and enable social interactions
  • Payment Processing: Facilitate Bitcoin/Lightning transactions for prizes and memberships
  • Service Improvement: Anonymous analytics to enhance app performance and features
  • Security: Prevent fraud and maintain platform integrity

We do NOT:

  • Sell your personal data to third parties
  • Use your data for advertising
  • Share sensitive information with external companies
  • Track your location beyond what's necessary for run verification

Payment Processing (Bitcoin/Lightning)

RUNSTR uses Bitcoin and the Lightning Network for all monetary transactions, providing enhanced privacy and security:

Bitcoin/Lightning Benefits

  • No traditional financial institution involvement
  • Pseudonymous transactions (no personal banking details required)
  • Fast, low-fee payments via Lightning Network
  • Self-custodial wallet support

Transaction Data

  • We do not store Bitcoin private keys or seed phrases
  • Lightning invoice creation and payment status only
  • Prize distribution records for competition verification
  • Subscription payment confirmations

Lightning Privacy

Lightning transactions are private by design. We cannot see the full payment path or your wallet balance - only the invoices you choose to pay.

Third-Party Services

We work with minimal third-party services, all chosen for their privacy-focused approach:

Nostr Network

  • Decentralized protocol for social features and identity
  • Your Nostr identity is pseudonymous and controlled by you
  • We relay messages but do not control or own your Nostr data

Lightning Service Providers

  • Used for Lightning Network payment processing only
  • We do not share personal data with these providers
  • Transactions are processed using Lightning invoices only

Analytics (Anonymous Only)

  • Crash reporting and performance monitoring
  • All data is anonymized before collection
  • No personal identifiers are included

Data Security

We implement robust security measures to protect your information:

Technical Safeguards

  • End-to-end encryption for sensitive communications
  • Secure data transmission using TLS/SSL
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Minimal data retention policies

Access Controls

  • Limited employee access to user data
  • Two-factor authentication for admin accounts
  • Regular access reviews and audits

Data Retention

  • Account data retained while your account is active
  • Competition results kept for historical records and verification
  • Technical logs deleted after 90 days
  • Account deletion permanently removes personal data

Your Rights

You have full control over your data and privacy on RUNSTR:

Access and Portability

  • View all data we have collected about you
  • Export your fitness data in standard formats
  • Download your competition history and achievements

Control and Deletion

  • Update or correct your profile information at any time
  • Delete your account and all associated data
  • Withdraw from specific competitions or teams
  • Opt out of non-essential communications

Privacy Settings

  • Control what fitness data is shared with teams
  • Set profile visibility preferences
  • Manage competition participation settings
  • Configure notification preferences

Your Data, Your Choice

With our local-first approach and Nostr integration, you maintain control over your identity and data. You can migrate to other compatible platforms at any time.
