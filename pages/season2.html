How Season 2 Works

1

Log Your Workouts

Use the RUNSTR app to log your running, walking, or cycling workouts. Tag a charity to contribute to their total distance.

2

Compete by Category

Each activity type (running, walking, cycling) has its own leaderboard. The charity with the most distance in each category wins ~166,666 sats.

3

Support Charity

Your workouts contribute to your chosen charity's total distance. Help them win the prize pool!
