Season 2 Registration

Entry Fee: 20,000 sats

How to Register

  1. Send 20,000 sats to: runstr@getalby.com
  2. Include your npub in the payment memo
  3. DM @RUNSTR on Nostr with your payment confirmation
  4. You'll be added to the participant list within 24 hours

What You Get

  • Compete for top 3 in Running, Walking, or Cycling
  • Support your chosen Bitcoin charity with every workout
  • Top 3 in each category receive RUNSTR merch
  • Winning charity receives 400,000 sats

How It Works

  • Log workouts using the RUNSTR app (Kind 1301 events)
  • Tag each workout with your chosen charity
  • Your distance is aggregated to both individual and charity leaderboards
  • The charity with the most collective distance wins!
