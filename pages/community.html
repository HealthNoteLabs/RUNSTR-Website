Community Guidelines

  • Be Respectful: Treat all community members with kindness and respect, regardless of fitness level or background.
  • Stay Positive: Encourage and support fellow athletes. Celebrate achievements and offer constructive motivation.
  • No Spam: Keep discussions relevant and avoid repetitive or promotional content not related to fitness.
  • Fair Play: Compete honestly and report any suspicious activity or cheating in challenges.
  • Privacy Respect: Don't share personal information of other members without their consent.
  • Appropriate Content: Keep all content family-friendly and suitable for all ages.
  • Help Others: Share knowledge, answer questions, and welcome new community members.
  • Follow Platform Rules: Adhere to the specific rules of each platform (Discord, Instagram, etc.).
