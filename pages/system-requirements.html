Additional Information

Internet Connection

Stable internet required for challenges, leaderboards, and data sync. Offline tracking available with sync when connected.

Battery Life

GPS tracking can impact battery life. We recommend keeping your device charged or using battery-saving mode for long activities.

Location Services

Precise location access required for accurate distance and route tracking. Background location needed for seamless tracking.

Storage Space

Additional space may be needed for activity data, cached maps, and app updates. Regular cleanup available in settings.

Device Compatibility Issues?

If your device doesn't meet these requirements or you're experiencing compatibility issues, contact our support team for assistance.
